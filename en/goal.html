Goal

 I believe that the advancement of IT technology is essential in making people's lives more convenient and comfortable. The first time I directly encountered IT technology was when I entered the university research lab in my fourth year. I have always been conscious of time, as I didn't like wasting time on things that would be unproductive. In that sense, I think IT technology is invaluable.
 In the world of research, it is common to use programming languages for data analysis in any field. I am no exception, as I have used Python, R, and MATLAB for analyzing experimental data. Through that, I experienced the sense of streamlining tasks using IT technology, which sparked my strong interest. As I delved deeper, I discovered the field of web development, which is widely used in our daily lives. As someone who always challenges things of interest, I pursued learning and development in this field, and I became passionately engrossed like never before.
 In the past, I made decisions about my university and major without much thought. I had good grades and put in more effort than others, but when asked if that was truly what I wanted to do, I couldn't answer with a definite "Yes." During my undergraduate years, I studied various subjects such as biology, real estate, accounting, finance, psychology, law, and obtained various certifications (a small brag: apparently, it's quite difficult to pass the real estate certification exam, but I managed to do it). Still, my future was uncertain, and I was contemplating whether I should follow the usual path of going to graduate school and getting a regular job. However, my perspective changed drastically in the year just before going to graduate school.
 I wholeheartedly dedicated myself to extracurricular activities, academics (biology), and certification exams. It is because of that, I can confidently declare my determination to pursue the path of an engineer. I want to become an engineer who never forgets the motto of making people's lives more convenient and contribute to society.
